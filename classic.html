Electronic Manufacturing Equipments

Various loaders and unloaders, including standard, CE type, single magazine, ergonomic, and boat handlers, streamline board handling, while vacuum pick-up loaders enable precise placement. Destackers separate stacked items, and conveyors are available in numerous types like gate link chain, cooling, inverter, and multi-angle turning. Buffer stackers, including SPI and AOI types, allow temporary board storage and inspection-related buffering. Cooling buffer conveyors manage board temperature during processing, and reject conveyors handle faulty boards. PCB cleaners maintain circuit board quality, and production machinery like laser and inkjet marking machines aid manufacturing. Additionally, inspection tools like barcode scanners and manual insertion lines complete the process, ensuring efficiency
